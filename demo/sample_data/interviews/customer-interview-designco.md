**Date:** 2026-02-22
**Interviewer:** PM Team
**Account:** DesignCo (SMB, $6k ARR)
**Context:** Churn risk — considering alternatives

## Key Quotes

> "We signed up for the real-time sync but honestly it feels like it's barely faster than Zapier now. Something changed in the last few months."

> "My team mostly uses it for Slack to Jira and vice versa. When it works, it's great. But the sync lag has been getting worse. Sometimes it takes a minute, sometimes five."

> "I can't justify the price if the core experience is degrading. We're a small team and every dollar matters."

> "The new features you keep announcing are nice, but I'd rather you fix the sync speed first. That's why we're here."

3. **Pricing flexibility** — per-connection pricing instead of flat tiers
