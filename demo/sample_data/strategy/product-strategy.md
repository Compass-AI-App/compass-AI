# SyncFlow Product Strategy — Q1 2026

## Vision

SyncFlow is the fastest way for teams to keep their work in sync across tools. Our vision is to become the default integration layer for modern teams.

## Strategic Priorities

### P0: Real-time Sync
Our core differentiator. All integrations should sync within 5 seconds. This is our moat — no competitor offers sub-10-second sync for all connectors.

### P1: Enterprise Security
SOC2 compliance, SSO, and audit logging. Required for landing enterprise deals. Target: complete by end of Q1.

### P2: Developer API
Open up our sync engine as an API. Early signals from 3 design partners. Target: beta by Q2.

## Key Metrics

- **North Star:** Weekly Active Synced Connections (target: 50k)
- **Revenue:** $2.5M ARR (target: $4M by EOY)
- **NPS:** 42 (target: 50)
