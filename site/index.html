How It Works

From raw evidence to agent-ready specs

Compass ingests your product's sources, finds where they conflict, and generates actionable specifications.

1

Connect & Ingest

Point Compass at your code, docs, analytics, interviews, and support tickets. It extracts structured evidence from each.

compass connect && compass ingest
2

Reconcile & Discover

Compass cross-references all sources to find conflicts, then ranks evidence-grounded product opportunities.

compass reconcile && compass discover
3

Specify & Build

Generate agent-ready specifications with task breakdowns, file paths, and acceptance criteria — formatted for Cursor or Claude Code.

compass specify "opportunity title"
Output

Evidence-grounded, never hallucinated

Every recommendation cites specific evidence. No generic advice. No hallucinated priorities.
